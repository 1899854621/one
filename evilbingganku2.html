E v i l的小饼干
The beauty in life is abundant, yet our current fast-paced lifestyle causes us to miss the kind of beauty that nourishes our souls. Perhaps, if only we would slow down and truly appreciate the beauty in everyday moments, our lives would no longer be confined to a monotonous routine of "three points in a line," but instead become a "real fairy tale" filled with beautiful elements and brimming with hopeful visions for the future.
生活中的美很多，但是由于我们现在的“快节奏”形式，使得我们错过了能够滋润我们“心灵”的美。 也许，只要我们慢下来，去欣赏那些生活中的美，我们的生活中不再是简单的“三点一线”，而是充满了“美好元素”的“真实童话”，充满了对未来的美好向往。
