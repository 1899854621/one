I know we will encounter many difficulties in real life, but not all of them are insurmountable. As long as we are willing, difficulties are merely a form of "alienation" of experience. I believe that you will never give up, you will face challenges head-on, and you will become the person you aspire to be!
我知道在现实生活中会遇到许多的困难，但是，并不是所有的困难都无法解决，只要我们愿意，困难就只是经验的一个“异化”。我相信，你们一定不会放弃，一定会迎难而上，一定会成为你想成为的人！
